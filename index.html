The Atelier Workshop
The Atelier Process

Where every stitch tells a story

We believe in slow craft. Each piece passes through the same hands from raw hide to finished object. No production lines. No shortcuts.

01

Leather Selection

Full-grain vegetable-tanned hides sourced from tanneries in France and Italy, chosen for grain, handle and durability.

02

Hand Cutting

Each panel is cut using solid brass patterns against a marble slab — perfect symmetry, zero compromise.

03

Saddle Stitching

Two-needle saddle stitch with waxed linen thread — the most durable method in leatherwork, pulled by hand.

04

Edge Burnishing

Every edge is bevelled, dyed, then burnished with a bone folder until it develops a smooth, glassy finish.
