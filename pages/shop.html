Maintenance

Aging gracefully

Full-grain leather doesn't wear out; it wears in. Without synthetic topcoats, the hide absorbs oils from your hands, sunlight, and friction to develop a rich, dark patina unique to your usage.

  • Brush regularly with horsehair
  • Condition deeply twice a year
  • Allow water spots to dry naturally
