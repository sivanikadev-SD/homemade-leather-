The Workshop

Planning your visit

01

Appointments Only

Because we are a working atelier, not a retail store, we require 48 hours notice to ensure an artisan is available to host you.

02

What to Expect

You'll tour the cutting room, see stitching in action, and spend time in our archive room reviewing leather swatches for your commission.

03

Parking & Access

We have two dedicated parking spots in the courtyard. The ground floor workshop is fully wheelchair accessible.
