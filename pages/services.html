What We Do

Services & Craftsmanship

From bespoke commissions to corporate gifting, our atelier offers a full range of leather craft services rooted in tradition.

Custom Commissions

Work one-on-one with our master craftsman to design a bag, wallet, or accessory built exactly to your specifications.

Lead time: 4–6 weeks

Monogramming

Hand-stamped initials, full names or custom logos. Available in blind, gold or silver foil. Perfect for gifts.

Available on all items

Corporate Gifting

Curated leather gift sets for teams and clients. Branded with your logo, packed in heritage boxes.

Min. order: 10 pieces

Restoration & Repair

Stitching, hardware replacement, edge re-finishing and deep conditioning to bring worn pieces back to life.

Free for Cordovan pieces

Wedding & Events

Bridal clutches, groomsmen wallets, and event favours as a complete coordinated set with custom dyeing.

Book 8+ weeks ahead

Craft Workshops

Half-day and full-day sessions at our atelier where you'll hand-stitch your own leather wallet. All materials included.

Every Saturday
How It Works

Our custom order process

1

Consultation

Share your brief — dimensions, purpose, leather preference. We advise and sketch initial concepts.

2

Design & Quote

Detailed drawings, material swatch card, and a fixed price quote within 48 hours.

3

Crafting

Your piece is crafted by a single artisan from start to finish, with photos at key stages.

4

Delivery

Delivered in our heritage gift box with a care card, authenticity certificate and artisan's signature.

Our Promise

What every client can count on

Lifetime Guarantee

Every Cordovan piece comes with lifetime repair. Stitching, hardware, edges — fixed free, forever.

Fixed Price Quotes

Your quote is your final price. We never add surprise charges mid-commission — what we quote is what you pay.

Progress Updates

We photograph your piece at every key stage and share them directly. You are never left wondering where things stand.

30-Day Returns

Return any ready-made piece within 30 days in original condition for a full refund. No questions asked.
